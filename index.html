CURRENT SITUATION

The Spartan Empire is a new country that has been threating various countries, ranging from Italy to Turkey.

ORIGINS

The first appearance of this country was made in the 20th of October 2012, during a protest in Athens. Many flags of the current country were spotted during that protest, but they were just regarded as symbolic flags at the time.
It got its independence in the 1st June of 2013, after countless riots in the Peloponnesian regions.

HISTORIC EVENTS

20-10-2012:

The Beginning of Spartan Riots

In that date, the Spartan riots for its independence began. Its first riot was in Sparta, and it was immediately turned into a violent riot. After a week the entire city of Sparta was occupied by the insurgents. Soon after 7 months the entire region of Peloponnese would be in the hands of the rioters.

05-06-2013:

Spartan Independence

When the Greek government realized it was too late to stop the insurgency, they gave the insurgents full independence with the Athens Agreement.

02-03-2015:

Spartan-Greek War

In that date, at exactly hour 22:59, unknown military forces began invading the Greek territories. This caused a full-scale conflict between Sparta and Greece, with also various countries joining in the war for support. Greece also requested support for NATO, but it was rejected since that it counted as an "internal war" for them.

06-09-2017:

Treaty of Patras

Greece capitulated at hour 21:59, after that they've realized most of their territories are occupied. In the following day at 07:00, the Treaty of Patras was signed. Its consequences of the treaty were:

  • Full annexation of Greece
  • Installation of a Pro-Spartan government in Albania
  • Annexation of a part of South-Eastern Macedonia
  • The former capital of Athens to be razed to the ground.

05-12-2020:

Instanbul Agreement

Sparta and Turkey made a secret agreement, which the only part we know is that Turkey leased their western lands to Greece. Other parts of the agreements are still unknown.
